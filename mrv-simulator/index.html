MRV Capture Protocol

  • Cryptographic Hashing

    Every submitted telemetry packet calculates a unique SHA-256-like hash of the entire payload state. This guarantees evidence remains untampered before entering the verification engine.

  • GPS Spoofing & Drift

    The system tracks the time and distance elapsed between submissions. Submitting telemetry from a location too far (>1km) in a short interval will flag an anomaly check.

  • Duplicate Image Defense

    If you upload an image with a signature already anchored on the ledger, the Verification engine will flag it as a duplicate credit fraud attempt.
